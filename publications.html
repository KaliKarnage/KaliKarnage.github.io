ARSIS 6.0: A Mixed Reality Application for Enhancing Astronaut Efficiency and Autonomy

Teogalbo, D. J. R., Auner, D., Ayala, N., Burnell, C., Dayrit, T., Gamel, G., Lotspeich, N., Smith, A., Swanson, S., Willerup, E., ... & Villanueva, B.

In Proceedings of the HCI International 2023 (pp. 5-9). Springer.

This research paper presents the development and implementation of ARSIS 6.0, a mixed reality application designed to enhance astronaut efficiency and autonomy during space missions. The system provides real-time information and guidance through an augmented reality interface, allowing astronauts to perform complex tasks with reduced dependency on ground control.

DOI: 10.1007/978-3-031-35634-6_27
