University Projects

360 Video Fishing Game

This was a group project during my sophmore year in university. Our task was to create an immersive interactive video using 360 video and the Unity engine. The final version of the project ran on the Oculus Quest 2 and the Quest Pro. I primarily worked on the models, textures, and the User Interface(UI). The models were made in Autodesk Maya and textured in Substance Painter. This was my first year using both Maya and Substance Painter so I took on these tasks to challenge myself to learn thses new tools and become more proficient in them.

C#
Maya
Substance Painter
Unity

Individual Immersive Game (Enigma)

This was a an individual game project made over the course of my time in the GIMM program at Boise State University. Our task was to create an immersive game in VR for the Oculus Quest 2 using all of our own assets. In our sophmore year were began working on our Game Design Documents for our games and then we began work on the games at the start of our junior year going to the end of our Senior year.

C#
Python

Mobile Data Narrative

This was an individual assignment to familiarize us with HTML, CSS, Javascript, React, and Node.js. Our task was to create a responsive website and gather data of our choosing and create three graphs us D3 to tell a story with. I used data on the weapons of World of Warcraft from the original release of the game in 2004 to the Legion expansion.

Javascript
